Its primary function is to provide a reliable and efficient way to generate PDFs from HTML, Markdown, and Office documents such as Word, Excel, and PowerPoint files.

This flexibility makes Gotenberg an ideal solution for a variety of use cases, including automated report generation, invoice creation, and the production of certificates or other official documents.
